Who We Are

More Than a Foundation But A Movement

Pad Up Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to restoring dignity and creating opportunities for girls by combating period poverty. Through menstrual hygiene education, advocacy, and the distribution of sanitary products, we help girls remain confident, healthy, and actively engaged in their education.

Every outreach program represents more than the donation of sanitary pads, it is an investment in education, confidence, health, and the limitless potential of every girl.
